How It Works

  • Step One

    Sign Up

    Sign up for an account - it is simple, easy, and free. All we need is the the usual login information (login, email, password), a quick email confirmation, and you are in.

  • Step Two

    Populate Resume

    Once you are signed up, you have be able to start adding content to your resume. The forms used to add the information are simple and intuitive. If you have a resume already with the content you want, you can copy/paste your way through our forms in a few minutes.

  • Step Three

    Publish Resume

    Once your resume has been fully populated, you can publish it with the click of a button. Publishing your resume immediately makes your resume available for review by all the companies that have accounts in this system (except those you indicated should not have access to it).

  • Step Four

    Get Contacted

    Now that your resume is published, you can sit back and let the employers contact you. The waiting can be difficult, so we have added some metrics so you know when your resume is viewed or liked by employers, as well as when your contact information has been purchased. This will give you confidence that you are getting the exposure you want from our employers.
