How It Works

  • Step One

    Sign Up

    Sign up for an account - it is simple, easy, and free. Besides the usual login information, all we need is the name of the company. No credit card information or anything.

  • Step Two

    Start Reviewing Resumes

    Once you are signed up, you have full access to all the resumes in the system, but you do not get access to the resume contact information. You can begin browsing and searching through them to find the ones you are interested in. You can also create filters to automatically find resumes of interest, and to receive email notifications when new resumes are published that match the created filters.

  • Step Three

    Interested in a Resume

    Eventually, you may find a resume of a candidate that you want to talk to and see about taking the next steps toward hiring them (phone screening, interviews, etc.). When this happens, then we have successfully added value for your company in your recruiting process. Because of this value, we ask you to purchase the contact information for the resume owner for a small fee.

  • Step Four

    Purchase Contact Information

    Purchasing the resume contact information is how this site earns revenue. We support a couple different secure payment options, and allow you to purchase "resume slots" in bulk if you wish.
